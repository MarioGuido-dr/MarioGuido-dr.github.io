## Law, Technology, and Cybercrime

*Module Development*

Development of a proposed undergraduate/postgraduate module examining legal responses to emerging technological challenges, including cybercrime, surveillance, artificial intelligence, and data privacy.

The project involves designing teaching materials and seminar structures to support student engagement with complex and evolving areas of law, with a focus on critical analysis and real-world application.
